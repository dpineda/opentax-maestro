## 3.2 SALT Deduction Cap Increase

* New cap: $40,000 ($20,000 MFS)
* Phaseout above MAGI $500,000 ($250,000 MFS)
* Floor remains $10,000

Expert Strategy:

## 3.3 Child Tax Credit (CTC) – 2025 Update

* Max CTC: $2,200
* Refundable ACTC: $1,700
* Valid SSN requirement enforced
* Joint return: one spouse must have valid SSN

Risk Review:

* ITIN-only dependents no longer eligible
* Validate issuance date before return due date

---

## 3.4 New Schedule 1-A Deductions (Major 2025 Change)

IA agents must master the following:

### A. No Tax on Tips

* Deduction up to $25,000
* Phaseout > $150,000 MAGI ($300,000 MFJ)
* SSN required
* Joint filing required if married

### B. No Tax on Overtime

* Deduct up to $12,500 ($25,000 MFJ)
* Same MAGI phaseout structure

### C. Car Loan Interest Deduction

* Up to $10,000
* Personal use vehicle purchased in 2025
* Phaseout > $100,000 ($200,000 MFJ)

### D. Enhanced Senior Deduction

* Born before January 2, 1961
* Up to $6,000 ($12,000 MFJ)
* MAGI phaseout > $75,000 ($150,000 MFJ)

IA Requirement:
Build automated MAGI sensitivity calculator.
