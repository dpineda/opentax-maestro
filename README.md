# OpenTax Maestro

**OpenTax Maestro** is an open-source **tax report learning assistant** designed to help individuals understand and practice the process of preparing personal income tax reports.

The goal of the project is educational: to **teach the structure, logic, and workflow of preparing personal tax filings step-by-step**. The name **“Maestro”** reflects the platform’s purpose — guiding users through the tax preparation process like a conductor guiding a performance.

OpenTax Maestro generates a **draft tax report** based on user-provided information and instructional guidance.
However, **all generated reports must be reviewed by a qualified tax professional before any official filing.**

---

## Project Goals

OpenTax Maestro is designed to:

* Teach individuals **how personal tax reporting works**
* Provide **step-by-step guidance** through the tax preparation process
* Help users understand **forms, deductions, credits, and filing requirements**
* Produce a **structured draft tax report** for educational review
* Encourage learning through **open-source transparency**

This project **does NOT replace professional tax preparation services**.
